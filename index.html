The One & Only Guacamole

"This is the best guacamole recipe you will ever find. Perfect for any revolution!" - Che Guevara

Time: 15 mins Serves: 2 Ingredients: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 quarter lemon wedge
  • chilli flakes
  • salt & pepper, to taste
  • Nachos or Doritos (or anything that sounds like it comes from Mexico).
  • Time and money
  • A house that preferbly has a kitchen

Method

  1. Slice an avocado in half, keeping the now-half avocado skin intact.
  2. Scoop out the avocado into a bowl, and mash it.
  3. Dice your tomato into small pieces and add it to your bowl.
  4. Squeeze your lemon juice into the bowl. High quality protective goggles are recommended in case lemon juice squirts into your eyes. Alternatively a welder's mask will work. If you don't have a welder's mask, then marry a welder.
  5. Add your chilli flakes to your liking. (If you're making this for somebody you hate, add the entire bag of flakes).
  6. Add salt-n-pepa to your liking. Would not recommend peaches and herbs though.
  7. Mix your ingredients. You can mash your guacamole further if needed.
  8. Fill the half-avocado skins with your guacamole, and then use them as bowls.Congratulations, you've saved on dishes.
  9. Enjoy with your choice of Doritos, or nachos.
